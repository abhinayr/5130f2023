Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the car leasing process work?

Car leasing involves renting a vehicle for a specific period with a fixed daily/monthly payment. When the lease term ends, you return the car or have the option to buy it.

2. What are the advantages of leasing a car?

Car leasing typically offers lower monthly payments. It can also include warranty coverage, reducing repair costs.

3. Are there mileage limits with car leases?

Yes, most car leases have mileage limits. Exceeding these limits may result in additional fees. Make sure to understand the terms of your lease agreement.

4. Can I customize a leased car?

Modifications to a leased car are typically not allowed. You should return the vehicle in its original condition to avoid penalties.
